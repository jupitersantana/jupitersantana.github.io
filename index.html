Who we are

Cubanita Frozen Foods is a family-owned, full-service dough manufacturer with over 42 years of experience. They provide high-quality empanada dough products to a wide range of customers nationwide, and are dedicated to satisfying their customers' needs and adapting to changing market demands.

Learn more about Cubanita Frozen Foods

What we offer

Our Cubanita Frozen Foods empanada product line offers multipurpose pastry dough (discos) that is perfect for a wide range of occasions. This versatile dough allows you to create a variety of pastries, from traditional empanadas to fruit pockets. Our flour-based discos come in two flavors: original and hojaldre, and are available in several sizes and shapes.
